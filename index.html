About Me

Hello! My name is Richard and I am currently in my second year at the University of Washington's Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering, majoring in Computer Engineering and minoring in Mathematics. I recently graduated in June 2023 from the Allen School Scholar's Program, formally, Allen School Startup Program. I grew up curious of how technology around us works and how I can use technology to make a change in the world.

I will be starting my new position as a Teaching Assistant at the Allen School for the Allen School Scholars Program in September 2023.

I am also currently on the lookout for an internship or study abroad opportunities to enhance my experience in the field and to improve my skills in coding and teaching others.
